About OSMS

Created in response to the COVID-19 pandemic, Open Source Medical Supplies helps local fabricators and institutions responsibly create and distribute protective gear and medical equipment to communities in crisis, providing medically and technically reviewed open-source design plans to fabrictors of all scales and guides to community organizers who want to help organize effective local support efforts for their region.

Facebook Page

Goal 1

Consolidate reviewed designs that can immediately be produced

Goal 2

Educate and connect volunteers to each other.

Goal 3

Offers support, mentors, and guides local communities as they alleviate the situation.

Open Designs

These are a collection of free and open source designs for PPE and medical equipment. The skills to make these vary from knowing how to use 3D printers or knowing how to sew fabric together. This collection has been curated to only include designs with finished prototypes. Some of these designs have been approved and are currently in use in hospitals.

PPE

Collection of PPE or personal protective equipment. PPE is essential in limiting the transmission of COVID19 to medical professionals.
