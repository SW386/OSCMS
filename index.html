About OSCMS

Coronavirus, COVID19, is officially a pandemic which means it affects everyone around the world. At the current rate, medical supplies- ventilators, masks, and PPEs- will fail to meet demand. This group and website aims to evaluate, design, and source fabrication of emergency medicine supplies around the world. More information here

Facebook Page

Goal 1

Consolidate designs that can immediately be produced

Goal 2

Education and connect volunteers to each other.

Goal 3

Offers support, mentors, and guides local communities as they alleviate the situation.

Open Designs

A collection of free and open source designs for a variety of medical equipment. The skills vary from know how to use 3D printers or knowing how to sew fabric together. The price point for the designs is also variable. This collection has been curated to only include designs with finished prototypes. Some of these designs have been approved and are currently in use in hospitals.

PPE

Collection of PPE or personal protective equipment, all of which have either been reviewed by medical professionals or are in hospital use. PPE is essential in limiting the transmission of COVID19 to medical professionals.

Medical Equipment

Collection of Open Source Medical Equipment including emergency ventillators, droplet barriers, and uvc-sterilization chambers all of which promote safe pulbic health measures.

Explore More
Filtered Face Masks

Collection of Open Source Face Mask designs which contain filters. Face masks are one of the first supplies to dwindle as they are essential to everyone, which underlines the need for innovative strategies.

Explore More
Face Shield

A collection of Open Source Face Shields. Face shields are often the first line of defense against pathogens, so strategies of maxinizing efficiency and reproducibility are always being developed.
